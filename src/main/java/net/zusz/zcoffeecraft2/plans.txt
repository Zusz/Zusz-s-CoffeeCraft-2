New Roast and Coffee system
-Arabica: time 2 strength 1
-Robusta time 1 strength 2
-Liberica time 1 strength 1 extra 1
-Excelsa time 2 strength 2 negative 1

Time 1 is 2.5 minutes
Time 2 is 4 minutes

-Light Roast: Base time, no delay
-Medium Roast: 2.5 -> 4 | 4 -> 5.5, 10sec delay
-Dark Roast: 4 -> 4.5 | 5.5 - > 6, 25 sec delay

-Adding sugar provides an extra minute of time (max 1)
-The extra effect of liberica is Speed for 2 minutes. In case of Speed being the base effect, 1 minute gets added to it
-The negative effect of Excelsa is 1 heart of damage on light, 1,5 on medium, and 2 on dark roast
